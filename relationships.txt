Relationships to extract


Example:
[
  ('United States', 'against', 'Dickinson'),
  ('United States v. Dickinson', 'plaintiff', 'United States'),
  ('United States v. Dickinson', 'defendant', 'United States'),
  ('United States v. Dickinson', 'court type', 'superior court'),
  ('United States v. Dickinson', 'court location', 'Arkansas'),
  ('Neely v. Robinson', 'references', 'Gilmore v. Pope')
]

Format:
[
   (CASE_NAME,      'decided on',     DATE),
   (PARTY,          'against',        PARTY),
   (CASE_NAME,      'plaintiff',      PARTY),
   (CASE_NAME,      'defendant',      PARTY),
   (CASE_NAME,      'court type',     COURT_TYPE),
   (CASE_NAME,      'court location', LOCATION),
   (CASE_NAME,      'references'',    CASE_NAME)
]

Potential relationships to add:
- Reason for the case
  PARTY - CHARGED WITH - CRIME

- Outcome of the case
